A: No! Our assessment is designed for everyone. The questions are in plain language, 
and the results are explained in non-technical terms.

Q: How long does the assessment take?
A: Typically 4-5 minutes. It's designed to be quick yet comprehensive.

Q: Can I retake the assessment?
A: Yes! You can take the assessment multiple times. Retaking it can show your progress 
over time as you implement recommendations.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. We take data security and privacy seriously. Your data is encrypted and stored securely.

Q: Are government grants really available?
A: Yes. Many governments offer AI adoption grants for SMBs. The specific programs depend

A: The assessment is completely free. We believe AI adoption should be accessible to all businesses.

Q: How can I use the roadmap?
A: Use it as your action plan. Start with quick wins, build momentum, and follow the 
prioritized steps. Each recommendation includes resources and guidance.
